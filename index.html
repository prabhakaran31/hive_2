About Us

Few Words About Us

Hive foundation is a Non profitable, Non Governmental Organization, aiming to provide care and compassion and extend its support to infected, destitute and vulnerable population.
Established in 2005, Hive has been committed to build capacity, livelihood and augment quality enhanced life years to its beneficiaries.
Hive foundation has adapted a slum area called Bharathi nagar in Tamil Nadu, India.
On January 2017, HIVE founded a community welfare center at Bharathi nagar.

Vision

Our vision is a world with equal access of education, health care and social justice to all age, castes and creeds. Until then we work towards acceptance and compassion for the infected and vulnerable, rejected and dejected, depressed and oppressed.

Mission

Our mission is HIVE- Helping the Infected and the Vulnerable Embrace life. Our Motto is Rejected to be elected and elevated.
